Machine Learning

Our team decided to create our project on one similar interest: The Stock Market. We built a model that uses Machine Learning and predicts whether a stock price will increase or decrease the next day. The prediction is based on the amount of volume exchanged by the end of a certain day and retrieves the opening and closing price of the stock and stores the difference. If the model predicts that stock price will go up, it buys it at the end of the day and sells right away the next day

Hack to the future

Our Project relates to the theme of the future by predicting future stocks. There is also a connection of future in technology as we used machine learning in order to acheieve our goal.
